APPLICATION DETAILS


This application is designed for bloggers . They can store their blogs permanently into this application. However they are free to change the content of blogs anytime they want. Initially you need to fill up the form by clicking on 'post a blog'. Then there you need to post the content of blog, category of blog, authors name and other details about the blog. After successfully posting the form, you can see blog card on the main screen. you can again enter into your blog by clicking on the the blog card. There you can edit it also. You can delete you blog also. you can permanently store as much as blog that you need

FACTS

Blogging is a very immediate medium: When you publish a post, it usually goes live on your blog right away. In fact, several things typically happen the moment you click the Publish button: The post appears at the top of your blog’s home page. The blogging software adds the post to your blog’s archive, usually by both date and subject, and to your RSS feed, which gets updated in newsreaders. Anyone who signed up for e-mail notifications receives an e-mail about your post. If your blog software pings blog search engines and services, those search engines and services receive a notice from your blog software that you’ve updated your blog and put that information into their catalogs. (A ping is simply an electronic notification.) Users of search engine websites can then get your blog post as a result of a search. A search-engine crawler indexes the post the next time it visits your blog. All these changes happen regardless of whether you think about them. The fact that blog posts are quickly distributed with a minimum of effort on the part of the blogger is part of the beauty and effectiveness of this format.
